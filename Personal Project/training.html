Start Your Session

Warm-up: Jump Rope – 5 minutes. Keep a steady rhythm, stay light on your feet, and focus on coordination and endurance.

Shadowboxing – 3 rounds. Work on proper technique, combinations, head movement, and footwork while imagining an opponent.

Footwork Drills – 5 minutes. Practice moving forward, backward, and side-to-side while maintaining balance and control.

Heavy Bag – 3 rounds. Focus on power, accuracy, and combining punches into fluid combinations with proper breathing.

Defense Practice. Work on slips, rolls, blocks, and maintaining a strong guard while moving and reacting.

Conditioning – push-ups, sit-ups, squats. Build strength, endurance, and overall body control for boxing performance.

Cool Down – light stretching. Relax your muscles, improve flexibility, and help your body recover after training.
