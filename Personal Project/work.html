Boxing starts with mastering the basics: stance, footwork, and punches. Every champion begins here.

Basic Punches

  • Jab: Fast and essential punch.
  • Cross: Strong straight punch.
  • Hook: Curved punch to the side.
  • Uppercut: Upward punch at close range.

Training Routine

  • Jump rope – 5 minutes
  • Shadowboxing – 3 rounds
  • Heavy bag – 3 rounds
  • Bodyweight exercises – push-ups, sit-ups, squats

Footwork, defense, equipment and tips

Move lightly stay balanced to control distance and avoid punches.
Keep your guard up, slip punches, block, and move your head.
Gloves, hand wraps, and a mouthguard are essential for safe training.
Focus on technique first, warm up properly, stay consistent, and listen to your body.

Don't forget:

Every champion starts at the beginning. Stay consistent, master the basics, and never stop improving. Every drop of sweat brings you closer to strength, speed, and confidence in the ring. Keep pushing — your dedication will define your success.
